Strategy and Governance are the cornerstones of any successful organisation.

Organisations struggle to define where they are, where they want to be and how they will get there. Building a strategy that not only secures your people, processes and technology but also supports your organisation’s goals is no easy task. There are pitfalls, realities, and biases in building a strategy such as resource constraints, internal politics and shortages of expertise that are hard to escape.

Green IT can help guide you through these pitfalls by taking a holistic and risk-based approach to defining and implementing the strategies required to secure your future.

Strategy requires knowing where you are, where you want to go, and what needs to be achieved to bridge that gap.

At Green IT we understand that defining the right strategy for your organisation, means finding an approach that works for you.

Define and Analyse Current State

Conduct a current state assessment against industry benchmarks, review existing controls and levels of risk against regulatory expectations, and identify critical assets that need to be protected.

Identify Desired Future State Outcomes

Work with leadership and business units to identify the objectives of the security strategy, the governance structures required, and future desired state.

Build a Roadmap to Success

Define the people, processes, technology that are required to achieve the desired future state.

Implement & Integrate

Green IT goes the extra mile as your trusted partner in delivering on the realisation of your strategy through our many service offerings –  CISO as a Service, Advisory Services, Engineering Services.

Service Offering

Green IT's Advisory team can help your organisation evaluate the effectiveness and efficiency of your cyber security programme. We can help develop an effective roadmap and governance model that supports your organisation's business goals. Green-IT offers a wide variety of strategy services including:

Design and develop a cyber strategy aligned to your organisation's goals and culture.
Assess and improve the governance of cyber security within your organisation.
Build and integrate SMART(Specific, Measurable,Attainable, Relevant, Timely) performance indicators into your governance procedures.
Assess your current cyber security programme against industry best practice and relevant regulations and compliance frameworks.
Define strategies for individual competencies such as - DLP, IAM, Resilience, Endpoint protection, etc.
