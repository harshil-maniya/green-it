Green IT bring a wealth of information security, technical operations, and project management industry experience to remove the complexities of security involvement in projects and technology transformations

“Security by Design” is often overlooked and avoided due to a perception that security is a blocker rather than an enabler in Digital Transformations.

Green IT’s Security Project Assurance professionals, engage with stakeholders across your organisation to ensure that your Information Security team’s interests are represented in all relevant projects and initiatives.

The Security Project Assurance function will facilitate a consistent and clearly defined Information Security contribution to the delivery of all projects so that project security requirements are identified, implemented, and formally documented for each project.

Our experienced professionals act as a trusted extension of your security team and a gatekeeper for projects being proposed and delivered. We not only solve the age-old problem of project management, resourcing, and skills within organisations, but also the complexities faced by organisations undertaking technical transformations and migrations to the cloud.

Service Offering

Our Security Project Assurance team partner with you to ensure that security supports projects and digital transformations. We ensure that the security components of projects are delivered in a better, faster, and more efficient manner which supports project success.

Our Security Project Assurance professionals work with your team to:

Establish proven processes and procedures within your organisation to build effective communication channels and collaboration between all of the necessary departments and stakeholders involved in new projects.
Understand the project scope, intent & impact, capture the goals and parameters.
Work with business stakeholders to identify opportunities to leverage existing technologies and align to existing policies and processes, whenever possible.
Understand the integration and implementation, consider control risks, ensure governance is applied and compliance is achieved, both internally and/or externally of your organisation.
Gain assurance that the appropriate security controls are implemented to manage the risks to an acceptable level.
Identify security requirements and confirm requirement closure or risk transfer before any project can progress to production.
Manage project closure, ensure complete transfer to business operations, including developing enduring requirements for future compliance to be maintained.
